Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Sage Season collects, how it is used, and what rights you have over your data. We keep this simple and honest — the same way we try to do everything.

Who we are

Sage Season is operated as an independent project. Our website is sageseason.com and you can reach us at hello@sageseason.com.

What information we collect

We only collect information you voluntarily give us:

  • Your first name and email address when you join our waitlist or founding circle via the form on our website.
  • Your responses to any optional questions we ask (such as "what do you most hope this helps with?").

We do not collect payment information, sensitive personal data, or any information through cookies or tracking beyond basic analytics (see below).

How we use your information

We use your name and email address to:

  • Send you your invitation when Sage Season opens
  • Share relevant updates about the product
  • Respond to any questions you send us

We will never sell your data, share it with third parties for marketing purposes, or use it for anything other than communicating with you about Sage Season.

Email marketing

By joining the waitlist you agree to receive emails from Sage Season about our launch and updates. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time and we will remove you promptly — no questions asked.

Our emails are sent via Kit (ConvertKit). You can read their privacy policy at convertkit.com/privacy.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely by Kit on servers in the United States. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information, though no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

We retain your data for as long as you remain subscribed, or until you ask us to delete it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (by unsubscribing)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at hello@sageseason.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Cookies and analytics

Our website does not currently use tracking cookies or advertising pixels. We may add basic, privacy-respecting analytics in future — if we do, we will update this policy.

Children's privacy

Sage Season is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy we will notify subscribers by email and update the "last updated" date at the top of this page.

Questions? We're at hello@sageseason.com — we actually read every email.
